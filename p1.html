Safe water protects and saves lives

Water.org has empowered more than 51 million people with access to safe water or sanitation through affordable financing.

Water is the way

We believe water is the best investment the world can make to improve health, empower women, enable access to education, increase family income and change lives. Yet, 771 million people lack access to safe water.
We’re working to change this. A global nonprofit organization working to bring water and sanitation to the world.

There's a smart way to end the water crisis

That’s where Water.org comes in. Water.org offers smart solutions that break down the financial barriers between people living in poverty and access to safe water and sanitation.
Our solutions are working! Today more than 51 million people around the world can turn on a tap or safely use a toilet because small, affordable loans empowered them to get access to safe water and sanitation at home.

Partnering to solve the water crisis

We are fortunate to work with partners – foundations, financial institutions, and brands – who share our passion and vision. Together, we are changing lives with safe water in 11 countries around the world.
